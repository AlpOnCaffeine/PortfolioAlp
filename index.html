About

Hello! I'm Anais Pease, a full-stack web developer based in Phoenix, AZ. With a degree from Arizona State University, I specialize in UI/UX design and am passionate about making technology accessible to everyone.
My approach to web development is rooted in a firm belief that empathy, sustainability, and artistry can coexist in the tech world to create not only functional but also beautiful virtual spaces. This philosophy guides my work, ensuring that each project I undertake is both aesthetically pleasing and optimally accessible.
Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of collaborating with innovative teams to deliver projects that prioritize user experience and environmental consciousness. My goal is to continue developing digital solutions that enhance user interaction through meticulous design and forward-thinking development practices.
If you're interested in creating digital experiences that bridge the gap between beauty and functionality, let's connect and explore how we can collaborate to make your vision a reality.
